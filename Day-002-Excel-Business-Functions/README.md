# 📊 Day 002 - Excel Business Functions

## 📌 Project Overview

This project demonstrates how Excel's logical and conditional functions can be used to solve real-world business problems using a retail sales dataset.

The objective was to classify sales transactions, generate business KPIs, and improve report readability using Excel's built-in functions.

---

## 🎯 Business Scenario

A retail sales manager wanted answers to the following questions:

- Which orders are High Sale and which are Low Sale?
- How many High Sale and Low Sale orders are there?
- What is the total revenue generated by High Sale orders?
- What is the average value of High Sale orders?
- How can important records be highlighted for faster analysis?

---

## 🛠️ Excel Functions Used

- IF()
- COUNTIF()
- SUMIF()
- AVERAGEIF()
- Conditional Formatting

---

## 📈 KPI Results

| KPI | Value |
|------|-------:|
| High Sale Orders | 1162 |
| Low Sale Orders | 8815 |
| Revenue from High Sale Orders | 1,474,984.10 |
| Average High Sale | 1269.35 |

Using Excel functions, thousands of sales records were automatically classified and summarized into meaningful business metrics.
